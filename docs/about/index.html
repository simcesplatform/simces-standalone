Research projects

SimCES and its parts have been developed in the following projects.

  • ProCemPlus, "Prosumer Centric Energy Communities – towards Energy Ecosystem", 2019-2021, funded by Business Finland

  • INTERRFACE, "TSO-DSO-Consumer INTERFACE aRchitecture to provide innovative grid services for an efficient power system", 2019-2022, funded by H2020 European Commission
